To My Boy Who Carries Whole Worlds Inside Him

March 25, 2026  ·  4 min read

✦

Mason, you are two years old and you already carry whole worlds inside you.

I see it when you go quiet in the middle of playing: not because you are bored, but because something has caught your attention and you are processing it somewhere deep and private, working it out before you bring it to the surface. I see it in the way you file new words away and then produce them days later, precisely, in exactly the right context. I see it when we go somewhere loud and crowded and you simply turn and walk away from it without any drama, as if you already know that certain environments are not for you and you have no interest in pretending otherwise.

There are people who will try to tell me that you need to "get used to" things that you instinctively know are wrong for you. I want you to know, for when you are old enough to understand this, that I have never once agreed with them. Your inner world is not a problem to be corrected. It is your greatest gift.

What I see in you

You are a thinker. A real one. You connect things and remember things and understand things at a speed and depth that catches me off guard regularly, and you are two. You love cars, and roads, and how things work, and the sound of music, and making coffee with Papa, and new words, and sand, and me reading to you before breakfast. You are happiest with a strong routine and at least one good adventure outside every morning.

You do not like chaos. You do not like crowds. You would rather go deep with one thing than skim across many. When you find something you love, you love it completely.

You are a genius in the quiet. My job is to hand you a torch so that the world can one day see what I already know is in there.

What I am here to do for you

I am not here to make you into someone louder or more social or more like what the world sometimes expects of children. I am here to make sure you have everything you need to bring what is inside you out into the light, when you are ready, in the way that is right for you.

That means I will read to you every single day. I will take you to the park in the morning sun. I will sit with you through the things that take patience and give you the space to process before you respond. I will not rush you. I will help you find your voice, not to perform, but because there are ideas inside you that the world genuinely needs to hear.

One day, when you are older, I hope you will read this and feel it: you were never a child who needed to be fixed or changed. You were a child who needed someone to see exactly who you were and love every single part of it. I hope I have done that. I hope I keep doing it every day.

You are my boy, and you are extraordinary. All my love, Mum x

✿ ✦ ✿ Made with love, for my two favourite people in the world.
